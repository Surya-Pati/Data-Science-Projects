# Sales & Profit Analytics Dashboard

This project delivers an interactive Excel dashboard to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer segments, and regional trends. The objective is to provide business stakeholders with clear, actionable insights for decision‑making and operational improvement.

---

## Project Overview

The Superstore dataset contains transactional sales records across multiple categories, regions, and customer segments. By leveraging Excel’s advanced features such as pivot tables, charts, and slicers, this dashboard enables dynamic exploration of KPIs and supports business intelligence reporting.

---

## Objectives

- Build an Excel dashboard to visualize sales and profitability trends  
- Segment performance by product category, customer type, and region  
- Provide interactive filters for dynamic analysis  
- Support business reporting and operational decision‑making  

---

## Dataset

- **Source:** Superstore dataset (publicly available or provided)  
- **Features:** Order ID, product category, sub‑category, sales, profit, discount, customer segment, region, and shipping details  
- **Target:** Business insights through dashboard visualization

---

## Methodology

1. **Data Preparation**  
   - Cleaning and formatting raw data  
   - Creating KPIs  

2. **Dashboard Development**  
   - Pivot tables for aggregation  
   - Charts for trend visualization  
   - Slicers for interactive filtering  

3. **Insights**  
   - Identification of high‑performing categories and regions  
   - Profitability analysis by customer segment  
   - Trend monitoring for operational planning  

---

## Results

- The dashboard provides a consolidated view of sales and profitability across multiple dimensions  
- Interactive filters allow stakeholders to drill down into specific categories, regions, or customer segments  
- Insights support strategic decision‑making and operational efficiency  

---

## Technologies Used

- Microsoft Excel  
- Pivot Tables, Charts, Slicers  
- Data Cleaning and KPI Calculation  

---

## How to Run

- Open the file:  
  `Superstore_Dataset_Insights_Dashboard.xlsx`

- Use the interactive dashboard tabs and slicers to explore sales and profitability insights.
