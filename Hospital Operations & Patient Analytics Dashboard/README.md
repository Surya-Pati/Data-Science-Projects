# Hospital Operations & Patient Analysis Dashboard

This project delivers an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze patient metrics, operational KPIs, and healthcare performance indicators. The objective is to provide healthcare administrators and analysts with actionable insights for improving patient care and operational efficiency.

---

## Project Overview

Healthcare organizations generate large volumes of data across patient records, treatments, and operational workflows. By leveraging Power BI, this project transforms raw data into interactive dashboards that highlight trends, KPIs, and performance metrics.  
The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor outcomes, identify inefficiencies, and support data‑driven decision‑making.

---

## Objectives

- Build a Power BI dashboard to visualize healthcare KPIs  
- Analyze patient demographics, treatment outcomes, and operational metrics  
- Provide interactive filters for dynamic exploration  
- Support healthcare administrators in improving efficiency and patient care  

---

## Dataset

- **Source:** Healthcare dataset (publicly available or provided)  
- **Features:** Patient demographics, treatment details, operational KPIs, hospital metrics  
- **Target:** Business insights through dashboard visualization

---

## Methodology

1. **Data Preparation**  
   - Cleaning and formatting raw healthcare data  
   - Creating calculated measures and KPIs  

2. **Dashboard Development**  
   - Interactive charts and tables  
   - Filters and slicers for dynamic exploration  
   - KPI cards for quick performance tracking  

3. **Insights**  
   - Patient demographics and treatment trends  
   - Operational efficiency analysis  
   - Performance monitoring for healthcare outcomes  

---

## Results

- The dashboard provides a consolidated view of patient metrics and operational KPIs  
- Interactive filters allow stakeholders to drill down into specific demographics, treatments, or operational areas  
- Insights support improved patient care and resource allocation  

---

## Technologies Used
