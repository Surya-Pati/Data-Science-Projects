# HR Attrition Analytics Dashboard

This project delivers an interactive Tableau dashboard to analyze employee attrition trends, demographics, and key drivers of turnover. The objective is to provide HR teams, business leaders, and analysts with actionable insights to improve retention strategies and workforce planning.

---

## Project Overview

Employee attrition is a critical challenge for organizations, impacting productivity, morale, and costs. By leveraging Tableau, this project transforms HR data into interactive dashboards that highlight attrition patterns and workforce dynamics.  
The dashboard enables stakeholders to explore attrition rates, demographic breakdowns, and satisfaction levels across departments and roles.

---

## Objectives

- Build a Tableau dashboard to visualize employee attrition trends  
- Analyze attrition across demographics, job roles, and departments  
- Provide interactive filters for dynamic exploration of HR metrics  
- Support HR decision‑making with actionable insights for retention strategies  

---

## Dataset

- **Source:** HR analytics dataset (publicly available or provided)  
- **Features:** Employee demographics, job roles, tenure, satisfaction levels, attrition status  
- **Target:** Workforce insights through dashboard visualization  

---

## Methodology

1. **Data Preparation**  
   - Cleaning and formatting HR data  
   - Creating calculated fields for attrition metrics  

2. **Dashboard Development**  
   - Interactive charts and tables  
   - Filters and parameters for dynamic exploration  
   - KPI cards for quick attrition tracking  

3. **Insights**  
   - Attrition trends across age groups, departments, and job roles  
   - Correlation between satisfaction, compensation, and turnover  
   - Workforce distribution and retention hotspots  

---

## Results

- The dashboard provides a consolidated view of employee attrition and workforce demographics  
- Interactive filters allow HR teams to drill down into specific roles, departments, or demographics  
- Insights support retention strategies, workforce planning, and employee engagement initiatives
